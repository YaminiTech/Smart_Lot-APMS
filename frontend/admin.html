Architect Pro: Master Mapping Guide

Mapping is the core of the intelligent system. Follow these precision controls:

  • SPOTS: Click 4 corners (top-left, top-right, bottom-right, bottom-left) to define an occupancy polymer.
  • NODES: Click once to place an anchor point. Use these for Entrances (Node 0), turns, and intersections.
  • PATHS: In Path mode, click a Start Node then an End Node to connect them. Connections are blue lanes.
  • END PATH: Use the 'End Current Path' button (sub-mode in Paths) or Right-Click to break a chain and start a new lane.
  • UNDO / CLEAR: Use 'Undo' to step back one point, or 'Wipe Mesh' to start the entire layout from scratch.

"Why is the AI detecting areas I didn't map?"

The AI model scans the whole frame but filters results based on your Polymers. Ensure your polygons are tight around the actual spot boundaries.

"How do I calibrate for different camera angles?"

Use the Map Architect in Settings. Points should be mapped from a high angle (birds-eye) for maximum accuracy.

"The video source says 'Standby'—how do I go live?"

Go to the Cameras tab and click 'Activate' on the source you want to broadcast to the system.
